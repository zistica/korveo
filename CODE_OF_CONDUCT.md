# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Korveo is an open, welcoming community. We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

English and Japanese both welcome. If you are more comfortable in Japanese — please use it.

## Our Standards

**Examples of behavior that makes this community better:**

- Being kind and respectful
- Giving constructive feedback on code, not the person
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Helping newcomers
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment of any kind
- Insulting or demeaning comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Dismissing someone because they are a beginner

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to:

**support@zistica.com**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban from the community, depending on severity.
